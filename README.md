# MANTO
Create an open-source text-to-video AI generation tool. 

The core purpose of `MANTO` is to be a storytelling tool. It is desinged to create an interactive narrative, animation, novel prompt-mechanism and open-weight model. In a nutshell, `MANTO` serves as an open-source competitor for the Gemini veo 3.

```Computer Science is the science of solving (hard) problems in general. If you automate CS, you trivially automate most of other professions. The only ones that won't budge are the regulated ones like lawyers, MDs and similar that have ways to prevent AI from wrecking their fields. Product/Project PMs are easy pickings. We'll also see wreckage coming to highly paid actors in the near future.```

I found (the aforementioned quote) in a hacker-news comment section. AI is not taking jobs but it is an auxiliary to the job market. It is a tool, a powerful tool that increases the efficiency and impact of each employee by three-folds. I always wanted to make films. Share captivating stories and make historical anecdotes fun and interesting. In short, I want to narrate important classical literature books into animated short stories. It is a prescient observation, that actors may get replaced with AI-models. These models can understand the script, the context and have the emotional depth that can be imitated. The brave new world is a plastic world. 

MANTO is designed to fill the gap. It must be better then the ponzi schemed `Flashloop` app. MANTO must be a programmable animation tool. There is no need to use third party apps to send prompts to generate animation. MANTO can simply do it for you.
